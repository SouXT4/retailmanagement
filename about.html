Get to know about our company

Two Guys Mart is a small convenience store established in 2022 within University Malaysia Sabah (UMS) Labuan. Operated as a partnership between two individuals, it caters mainly to UMS students living nearby, offering daily necessities like snacks, beverages, toiletries, and groceries. The store benefits from its strategic location close to student housing, ensuring steady foot traffic. Currently, it has only one physical outlet and has not expanded, though the partners are considering new locations. There is an unrelated store with a similar name near Lab Point Hotel.
